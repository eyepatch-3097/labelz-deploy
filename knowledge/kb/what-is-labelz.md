# What is Labelz?

**Labelz** is a DIY label and tag creation tool for small brands. It helps you **design label templates** and then **generate labels (single or bulk)** with **serialized codes** like **Barcodes** and **QR codes**—so you can tag, pack, ship, and track products with fewer manual steps.

Think of Labelz as your **one-stop workspace for product tags + packaging labels**, where you can keep your designs consistent and generate print-ready outputs whenever you need them.

---

## Who Labelz is for

Labelz is built for:
- **D2C founders and small brands** who need professional labels without complex software
- **Boutique manufacturers / sellers** managing SKUs, variants, and repeat production runs
- **Handmade sellers** (crochet, gifting, home decor) who want branded tags and packaging
- **Growing teams** that need shared templates across multiple members or workspaces

---

## What you can do with Labelz

### 1) Create a structured workspace for labels
- Create an **Org** for your company/brand
- Create **Workspaces** (e.g., “Winter Collection”, “Amazon Packaging”, “Retail Tags”)
- Keep templates and batches organized per team or purpose

### 2) Design reusable label templates
- Build a **Template Design** once and reuse it anytime
- Add brand elements like:
  - logo, colors, marketing content
  - product fields (SKU, MRP, size, material, etc.)
  - packaging information (manufactured date, batch, net qty, etc.)
- Keep layouts consistent across products and collections

### 3) Generate labels (single or bulk) with serialization
- Generate **one label** quickly for a new product
- Generate **bulk labels** for a full production run
- Create **serialized labels** (unique identifiers) for:
  - barcode/QR-based scanning
  - inventory workflows
  - batch-level tracking and reprints

### 4) Print-ready outputs for real-world packaging
Physical products need clean printing. Labelz focuses on making the output usable for the real world:
- print-ready label layouts on any printer (thermal, standard sheets)
- consistent formatting
- bulk generation to reduce manual work

---

## What Labelz is not

To set the right expectation:
- Labelz is **not** a full inventory/WMS/OMS system
- Labelz does **not** replace your marketplace catalog tools
- Labelz is primarily focused on **label design + label generation + serialization**, not end-to-end supply chain

---

## Key concepts (simple definitions)

### Org
Your company/brand account inside Labelz. Orgs help keep your templates, workspaces, and usage tied to one business.

### Workspace
A workspace is a folder-like area inside an Org used to organize templates and generation runs. Many teams create separate workspaces for different product lines or channels.

### Template Design
A reusable label layout you create once (design + fields + formatting). You can generate labels from a template many times.

### Batch (Bulk Generation)
A batch is a bulk label generation run—usually for production or restocking—where you generate many labels in one go.

### Serialization
A way to ensure each label gets a unique code (like a sequential number, barcode, or QR) so each item can be uniquely identified.

## Common use cases

### Fashion 7 Apparel / Footwear
- garment tags with SKU, size, color, MRP, care instructions
- packaging labels for shipping or retail

### Beauty / Cosmetics
- product labels with shade, net quantity, batch, MFD/EXP, MRP
- carton labels and variant-level labeling

### Food / Consumables
- packaging labels with net weight, batch, MFD/EXP, pricing
- barcode/QR-based product identification

### Handmade / Gifting / Home Decor
- branded tags, thank-you labels, packaging stickers
- simple bulk generation for small-batch production

### Jewellery
- branded mini tags on each SKU
- thermal print for small labels, packaging stickers
