# Pricing and Plans for Labelz

Labelz offers simple plans so you can start small and scale as your label volume grows.

This page explains:
- what plans typically include
- what limits you should expect
- how to choose the right plan
- billing and invoice basics

---

## How Labelz pricing usually works

Labelz pricing is designed around a few core levers:

### 1) Workspaces
How many separate work areas you can create inside an Org. 
**Why it matters:** Workspaces keep templates and batches organized by team, channel, or product line.

---

### 2) Templates / Template Designs
How many reusable templates your Org can create.

**Why it matters:** Templates are your reusable assets—more templates usually means more product categories, label sizes, or channel-specific formats.

---

### 3) Label generation volume (credits or usage)
How many labels you can geneerate monthly.
**Why it matters:** This controls how much you can print at scale.

---

## Typical plan tiers (guidance)

### Starter (best for early-stage brands) - $39
Recommended if you:
- generate labels occasionally
- have a small catalog
- want 1–2 workspaces and a few templates

Usually includes:
- 1 Org, 1 Workspace
- Upto 3 templates 
- single and bulk generation (with basic limits) - Upto 3000 total label generations
- basic history and reprints

---

### Pro (best for scaling D2C) - $69
Recommended if you:
- have multiple product lines or channels
- print labels frequently
- want more templates and larger bulk runs

Usually includes:
- higher label generation volume - 30000 total label generations
- Unlimited workspaces & Unlimited templates
- Priority support
- stronger history tracking for reprints

---

### Custom / Super (best for high-volume operations)
Recommended if you:
- run daily production/dispatch
- need multiple users with controlled permissions
- require large batch generation volumes

Usually includes:
- Custom generation limits
- advanced access control patterns
- API Support for webhooks
- priority support (if enabled)
- better audit/history visibility

---

## How to choose the right plan (simple rules)

### If you are testing Labelz
Choose Starter:
- create 1–3 templates
- run small single/bulk tests
- validate print and scanning workflows

### If you are printing weekly (or managing many SKUs)
Choose Pro:
- multiple templates by category
- ongoing batch generation
- less manual work

### If you are printing daily / in large batches
Choose Business/Super:
- large bulk runs
- multiple operators
- controlled permissions and history for reprints

## Billing and invoices

### Do you provide invoices?
Yes, Labelz typically supports invoice generation for paid plans.

### Where can I view invoices?
Go to **Billing → Billing History / Invoices** (Admins only).

### What happens if a payment fails?
Your plan may not update. Check **Billing History**, then email support with the payment reference if needed.

---

## Refunds and cancellations
Refund and cancellation rules can depend on the plan and billing cycle.

If you need to cancel, refund, or change a plan:
Email **shyama@dotswitch.space** with:
- Org name
- plan name (if known)
- reason for cancellation/change
- invoice reference (if applicable)

---

Email **shyama@dotswitch.space** with:
- your industry
- number of SKUs

- team size (how many people will use Labelz)
