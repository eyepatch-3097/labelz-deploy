# Workflow: Org Creation in Labelz

An **Org** is your company/brand account inside Labelz. Everything you do—**workspaces, templates, label generation, members, and billing**—is managed under an Org.

This guide walks you through creating your Org and setting it up correctly from day one.

---

## Before you start (recommended)
Have these ready:
- Your **brand / business name** (as you want it to appear on invoices and inside the app)
- The **primary email** for the Org admin
- A quick idea of how you want to organize workspaces (optional, but helpful)

---

## Step-by-step: Create your Org

### Step 1 — Sign up / log in
1. Open **Labelz**
2. Sign up (if new) or log in with your account
3. You’ll land on the onboarding / home flow

---

### Step 2 — Create a new Org
1. Click **Create Org** (or similar CTA shown during onboarding)
2. Enter:
   - **Org name** (usually your brand / company name)
   - (Optional) brand details shown inside the product if available (logo/name formatting)

3. Click **Create**

✅ Result: Your Org is created and becomes your default “home” in Labelz.

---

### Step 3 — Confirm your Org context
After Org creation, confirm:
- You are inside the correct Org
- You can access Org settings (typically visible to admins)

This matters if you plan to create multiple Orgs (e.g., separate for multiple brands).

---

## Best practices for Org setup

### Keep Org = Company (not collections)
Use **one Org per company/brand**, and then use workspaces for:
- collections
- channels (Amazon / retail / wholesale)
- teams

---

## Common mistakes (and how to avoid them)

### “I created an Org with the wrong name”
If Labelz allows Org rename, update it in **Org Settings**.  
If rename is not available, contact support with:
- old org name
- new org name
- your login email

### “I want multiple teams—should I create multiple Orgs?”
Usually no. Prefer:
- **1 Org**
- multiple **Workspaces**
- **Access control** for different team members

---

## Troubleshooting

### I don’t see the Create Org option
- Ensure you’re logged in
- Refresh once
- Try from a different browser if the UI is stuck

If it still doesn’t show, email **shyama@dotswitch.space** with:
- your email
- screenshot of the page you’re on
- what you expected to see
