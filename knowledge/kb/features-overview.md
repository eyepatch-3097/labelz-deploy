# Labelz Features Overview

This page gives you a complete view of what you can do inside **Labelz**—from setting up your Org to designing templates, generating labels (single or bulk), managing access, checking history, and handling billing.

---

## 1) Org creation

An **Org** is your company/brand account inside Labelz. Everything you do—workspaces, templates, label batches, and billing—lives inside an Org.

### What you can do
- Create an Org for your brand/business
- Set basic Org details (name, brand identity, etc.)
- Use one Org with multiple workspaces for different teams or channels

### Why it matters
- Keeps your label work centralized
- Makes it easy to manage members, permissions, and billing in one place

---

## 2) Workspace setup (recommended structure)

A **Workspace** is how you organize your work inside an Org.

### What you can do
- Create multiple workspaces for different purposes, like:
  - `Retail Tags`
  - `Amazon Packaging`
  - `Wholesale Cartons`
  - `Festive Collection 2026`
- Keep templates and generation runs (batches) neatly separated

### Why it matters
- Prevents template clutter
- Helps teams work independently without mixing assets

---

## 3) Template creation

A **Template** is a reusable starting point for generating labels. You create a template once and reuse it for different products or production runs.

### What you can do
- Create templates per use case:
  - product tag template
  - pouch/packaging label template
  - carton label template
- Add structure via fields (SKU, MRP, size, etc.)
- Save templates for repeat use

### Output
A template becomes the “base” from which you generate labels—single or bulk.

---

## 4) Template design (layout + fields + branding)

A **Template Design** is where the actual layout is built—how your label looks when printed.

### What you can design
- **Branding**
  - logo
  - brand text
  - colors and styling
- **Product fields**
  - SKU, product name, variant, size, material, color
  - pricing (MRP, offer price)
- **Compliance / packaging fields** (industry-dependent)
  - net quantity, batch, MFD/EXP, manufacturing details
- **Codes for scanning**
  - Barcode
  - QR code
  - serialized identifiers (unique codes per label)

### Why it matters
Physical products require print-ready layouts. Template Design helps you build something consistent that can be reused at scale.

---

## 5) Single label generation

Single label generation is best when:
- you’re testing a new template
- you need a quick label for a one-off item
- you’re iterating on design before bulk printing

### What you can do
- Select a template
- Fill in the required fields (manually or via data entry)
- Generate a print-ready label output

### Typical scenarios
- prototype / sampling labels
- urgent replacement labels
- small-batch handmade items

---

## 6) Bulk label generation (Batch generation)

Bulk generation is for scale—when you need tens, hundreds, or thousands of labels in one go.

### What you can do
- Generate a **Batch**
- Provide bulk data (for multiple SKUs/variants/items)
- Generate labels with **serialization** (unique IDs per label)
- Create print-ready outputs suitable for production workflows

### Why it matters
- Saves time vs generating labels one by one
- Reduces manual errors
- Supports repeat production and reprints

---

## 7) Access control (team + roles)

Labelz supports team usage via access control so work doesn’t break when multiple people are involved.

### What you can do (typical access patterns)
- Invite members to your Org / workspace
- Limit who can:
  - create/edit templates
  - generate labels/batches
  - view history
  - manage billing

### Why it matters
- Prevents accidental template edits
- Helps separate responsibilities (designer vs ops vs admin)
- Keeps billing and Org-level actions restricted to admins

> Note: The exact roles and permissions depend on your plan and configuration.

---

## 8) Label history (audit + reprints)

Label history helps you track what has already been generated so you can:
- reprint without recreating
- trace older batches
- troubleshoot issues

### What you can typically view
- past single-label generations
- bulk batches and their outputs
- template used + time of generation
- status (completed / failed if applicable)

### Why it matters
In real operations, most issues are solved faster when you can see “what happened last time”.

---

## 9) Billing & plans

Billing controls what plan your Org is on and what limits apply.

### What you can do
- View current plan
- Upgrade/downgrade (if enabled)
- View invoices and payment history
- Track plan limits (workspaces/templates/credits/usage depending on plan)

### What users often ask
- “Which plan should I take?”
- “What happens when I hit limits?”
- “Do you support invoices for accounting?”
- “Can I cancel anytime?”

---

## Frequently asked questions

### Is Labelz only for barcodes and QR codes?
No. Labelz supports multiple label types—including product tags and packaging labels—where you can include branding elements, product data, and marketing collateral.

### Can I use Labelz for different industries?
Yes. Labelz is commonly used across fashion, beauty, food, handmade/gifting, and home decor. The exact fields required depend on your industry and where you sell.
