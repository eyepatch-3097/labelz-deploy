# Billing History in Labelz (Invoices, Payments, and Plan Tracking)

Billing History helps you view and manage your organization’s payments, invoices, and plan-related records in Labelz.

This is useful for:
- accounting and GST/compliance workflows (if applicable)
- tracking plan upgrades/downgrades
- verifying payment status
- downloading invoices for records

---

## Who can access Billing History?
Billing is a sensitive area and should typically be accessible only to:
- **Org Owner / Admin**

If you cannot see billing pages, you may not have admin permissions.

---

## What you can typically see in Billing History

### 1) Current plan details
- plan name
- renewal/cycle (monthly/annual, if applicable)
- included limits (templates/workspaces/credits depending on plan)
- current usage snapshot (plan-dependent)

### 2) Invoice list
For each invoice/payment record you may see:
- invoice number
- date
- amount
- status (paid / pending / failed)
- download link (PDF)
- payment method reference (if applicable)

### 3) Payment records
- successful payments
- failed or incomplete payments
- refunds (if supported)

---

## Common billing workflows

### A) Download an invoice
1. Go to **Billing**
2. Open **Billing History / Invoices**
3. Select the invoice
4. Download PDF

✅ Tip: Keep invoices stored in your accounting drive for easy audit.

---

### B) Verify payment status
If your plan didn’t update after payment:
1. Check the latest payment record in Billing History
2. Confirm status:
   - Paid ✅
   - Failed ❌
   - Pending ⏳

If the status looks correct but the plan is not updated, contact support.

---

### C) Track upgrades/downgrades
Billing History helps you confirm:
- when the plan changed
- what invoice was generated for the change
- which cycle it applies to

---

## Troubleshooting

### “Payment succeeded but plan didn’t change”
Email **shyama@dotswitch.space** with:
- Org name
- payment date/time
- payment amount
- invoice/payment reference (screenshot is fine)

### “I don’t see invoices”
Possible reasons:
- you are not Org Admin
- invoice generation may be pending
- you are in the wrong Org

### “I need invoice details changed”
If you need invoice info (company name, GST, billing address) updated, email support with:
- Org name
- what needs correction
- invoice number (if already generated)

---
