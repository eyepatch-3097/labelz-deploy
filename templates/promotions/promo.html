Frequently Asked Questions

What types of labels can I create?
You can create product tags, shipping labels, barcode stickers, ingredient lists, and custom packaging labels of any exact dimensions you need for your thermal or standard printers.
Can I generate unique serialized labels in bulk?
Yes. You can upload a CSV or Excel file to instantly bulk-generate thousands of labels, each with unique serial numbers, dynamic pricing, or specific barcodes.
Do I need design skills?
Not at all. Our drag-and-drop editor is built specifically for operations teams and founders, not graphic designers. If you can use a basic document editor, you can use Labelz.
Can I use barcode and QR codes together?
Absolutely. You can add multiple dynamic barcodes and QR codes on a single label template, mapped to different data points in your upload file.
What happens after I subscribe?
You get instant access to the platform. You can immediately set up your first Organization workspace, define your base label dimensions, and start designing and generating labels.
Do I get onboarding help?
Yes, our Launch Plan includes dedicated support to help you set up your first templates and ensure your workflows are running smoothly.
