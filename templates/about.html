Hey there,

We’re here to “simplify” commerce.

Let’s be honest. Labelz isn't some deep-tech paradigm shift. It’s an attempt to stop pulling our hair out over something that should be simple.

Commerce has always been a game of revenue vs supply costs. Over time, bizarrely complex regulatory hurdles and clunky enterprise software have crept in. This has made a mess of the basics.

"Honestly? We just wanted people to stop making labels manually on spreadsheets."

The Founders

Unlike pure software, physical products have a tangible journey. You see them, feel them, smell them. They go from a production line, to a cardboard box, to a dusty warehouse shelf, into a delivery van, and eventually, they land on your customer's table.

In order to simplify this journey, there are a million critical touchpoints where tools come in to help you brand, label, pack, ship, and deliver. We, like many others, are just one piece of that puzzle.

"It's not just a sticker. A label is the very first thing your customer sees when they finally hold what they paid for."

Our Philosophy

What makes us different is our obsession with experience triggers. We don't just build features; we look for the exact moments where the operational journey breaks for you, or where the unboxing experience breaks for your customer. We want to make that exchange feel fair, seamless, and professional.

Labelling and packaging serialized barcodes is just the medium. Labelz is our first serious shot at solving a very real, very annoying commercial pain point.

But design isn't our only moat. We wanted to make sure our clients can use the platform as an all-purpose tool. So we always knew serialization & inventory tracking are non-negotiable. Every piece of label printed with Labelz, is unique, GLOBALLY. That means 4 packs of your product can fly off the shelf to 4 different locations and you will know exactly where each of them went.

Every warehouse, Every store, Every customer - tracked. Every pack, every unit inside the pack, every box - tracked. As long as the labels were scanned.

"Globally Unique how? What does Labelz do?"

Our Clients Ask

Every label designed on Labelz is a story. It is more than just product details. It covers your branding, your marketing pieces, your operations with your labels. Each EAN in every label, is made unique and trackable by us with serialization logic. Marketing items can be embedded into the design & your QR. Trackable codes can be imported to your ERP. If you can imagine it and your brand needs it, we already solved it for you. Or probably will find a way to do it, that's our promise.

We hope you feel the care we've poured into this product. We're going to keep iterating, simplifying, and repeating until labeling is the easiest part of your day.

Onwards and upwards, always.
— The Labelz Team
