What is Labelz?

Labelz is a label creation and bulk generation platform for brands needing print-ready labels quickly and consistently.

  • Create templates once (layout, fields, styling).
  • Generate single or bulk labels with serialized codes.
  • Manage labels across teams with Org Workspaces.
  • Print/export labels reliably for daily ops.
